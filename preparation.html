Step 1: Preheat oven to 200 degrees C. Line a baking pan with parchment paper and set aside.
Step 2: Cream the softened butter.
Step 3: Add white, brown and vanilla sugar, water and the egg to the butter. Mix until fully combined.
Step 4: While stirring add flour, baking powder and salt little by little.
Step 5: Add the chocolate chips and walnuts and mix until fully combined.
Step 6: With a teaspoon form small balls of dough and place them 6cm apart from each other on the tray.
Step 7: Bake for 10 mins and let them cool down. And Enjoy!
